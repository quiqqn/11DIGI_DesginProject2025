Purpose:

The purpose of this website is to showcase the diverse and delicious recipes from East Asia, focusing on Chinese, Japanese, and Korean cuisines. Each section will provide a glimpse into the unique flavors and cooking techniques that define these culinary traditions. Showcase and teach students how to cook cuisines from China, Japan and Korea, allowing them to learn and explore these cultural foods. It will contain a page for each culture that showcases food that they can choose from, then going to a recipe page for that selected recipe and teach them how to make it with images and instructions.
