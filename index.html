Purpose:

The purpose of this website is to showcase the diverse and delicious recipes from East Asia, focusing on Chinese, Japanese, and Korean cuisines.

Each section will provide a glimpse into the unique flavors and cooking techniques of these special cultures. It is expecting that you know how to cook and are familar with cooking terminolgy as the website is intended to for users to learn about three different Asian cultures cuisines, rather than teach you how to cook from scratch.
